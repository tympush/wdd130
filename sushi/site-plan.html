Content

Home page

Welcome to Fishron Sushi your go-to destination for the finest sushi in Belgium. Our commitment to serving authentic Japanese cuisine with a modern twist sets us apart. From traditional sushi to innovative creations, we offer a culinary experience that will tantalize your taste buds.

Experience the art of sushi-making as our skilled chefs meticulously craft each roll, combining the freshest ingredients with precision and creativity. At Fishron Sushi we take pride in delivering the perfect fusion of taste and presentation, making every visit a memorable one.

Immerse yourself in the inviting ambiance of our restaurant, where contemporary design meets the warmth of Japanese tradition. Our restaurant provides a cozy and elegant atmosphere, making it an ideal place for intimate dinners, special celebrations, or casual gatherings.

We believe that every visit to Fishron Sushi should be an experience that engages all your senses. Our background music and lighting are thoughtfully chosen to create an atmosphere that's both soothing and invigorating, enhancing your overall enjoyment.

Our Restaurant

Meet our team of dedicated sushi chefs, each with a deep passion for Japanese culinary traditions. Our experienced chefs have honed their skills over years of training and take pride in delivering sushi that's not only delicious but also beautifully presented.

Located in the heart of Gent, our restaurant boasts a welcoming and spacious interior with an authentic Japanese touch. Our wooden tables and traditional d√©cor provide the perfect backdrop for an unforgettable dining experience.

We believe in the importance of sustainability, which is why we source our ingredients responsibly. Our commitment to using fresh, high-quality seafood and locally-sourced produce ensures that every dish served at Fishron Sushi reflects our dedication to both taste and environmental consciousness.

Our Menu

Individual Sushi:

Delight in a diverse selection of individual sushi pieces, where you can savor the purity of each ingredient. From classic options like salmon, tuna, and eel, to unique creations like our signature rolls, our separate sushi menu offers a variety that caters to all palates.

We take pride in our sushi's authenticity, with rice perfectly seasoned and fish sliced with precision. Each piece is a masterpiece on its own, allowing you to explore the distinct flavors and textures of different fish and seafood.

Don't miss our special breaded rolls, a fusion of Japanese and Western influences. These rolls feature a crispy, panko-breaded exterior, enveloping the delicate flavors of our handcrafted sushi. From the Crispy Spicy Tuna Roll to the Tempura California Roll, they offer a satisfying contrast of textures that will tantalize your taste buds. These special rolls provide a unique twist on the traditional sushi experience, delivering a delightful mix of crunch and freshness.

Sushi Sets:

For a complete sushi experience, explore our thoughtfully curated sushi sets. Our sets offer a combination of flavors and textures that harmonize beautifully, providing an opportunity to enjoy a selection of sushi in one elegant presentation.

Whether you're a sushi connoisseur or a first-time visitor, our sushi sets are designed to cater to your preferences. From the Chef's Selection to vegetarian options, each set is crafted to satisfy your sushi cravings and introduce you to new tastes.

Experience a delightful array of flavors with our family sets, tailored for group dining. These generously portioned platters feature a diverse selection of nigiri, sashimi, and specialty rolls, making them perfect for family gatherings, friendly get-togethers, or any special occasion. From the colorful Rainbow Roll to the exquisite sashimi, our family sets showcase our dedication to quality and taste, ensuring a memorable group dining experience.
