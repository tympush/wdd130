Our Menu

Individual Sushi:

Delight in a diverse selection of individual sushi pieces, where you can savor the purity of each ingredient. From classic options like salmon, tuna, and eel, to unique creations like our signature rolls, our separate sushi menu offers a variety that caters to all palates.

We take pride in our sushi's authenticity, with rice perfectly seasoned and fish sliced with precision. Each piece is a masterpiece on its own, allowing you to explore the distinct flavors and textures of different fish and seafood.

Don't miss our special breaded rolls, a fusion of Japanese and Western influences. These rolls feature a crispy, panko-breaded exterior, enveloping the delicate flavors of our handcrafted sushi. From the Crispy Spicy Tuna Roll to the Tempura California Roll, they offer a satisfying contrast of textures that will tantalize your taste buds. These special rolls provide a unique twist on the traditional sushi experience, delivering a delightful mix of crunch and freshness.

Sushi Sets:

For a complete sushi experience, explore our thoughtfully curated sushi sets. Our sets offer a combination of flavors and textures that harmonize beautifully, providing an opportunity to enjoy a selection of sushi in one elegant presentation.

Whether you're a sushi connoisseur or a first-time visitor, our sushi sets are designed to cater to your preferences. From the Chef's Selection to vegetarian options, each set is crafted to satisfy your sushi cravings and introduce you to new tastes.

Experience a delightful array of flavors with our family sets, tailored for group dining. These generously portioned platters feature a diverse selection of nigiri, sashimi, and specialty rolls, making them perfect for family gatherings, friendly get-togethers, or any special occasion. From the colorful Rainbow Roll to the exquisite sashimi, our family sets showcase our dedication to quality and taste, ensuring a memorable group dining experience.
